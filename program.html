ITI CHINNARALAYA - ITI Creche And Baby Day Care Center








Infant Care

Age group- 6 months and above
First year of a child is the most important phase of development where they grow in leaps and bounds, At Chinnaralaya, we provide a loving,creative and nurturing environment to help our littlest learners' minds and bodies develop

Pre-Nursery

Age group- Must be 18 months or more as of june 1st
Or Pre-Nursery Program encourages children to use their senses, inquisitive nature and their emerging problem solving skills to learn about and make sense of their world. We Offer a mix of individual and group activities to ensure that your toddler is using all their energy in productive ways.

Nursery

Age group- Must be 2 years 6 months or more as of june 1st
Nursery children are enthusiastic learners and are constantly refining and expanding their knowledge and skills. We provide innumerable opportunities to explore, try new things and grow. Pre-writing and reading skills, pre-math concepts are all introduced to cater to their academic learning.

After School

Age group- 3 years 6 months to 10 years
After school program is divided into 2-
1) Junior After school - 3 years 6 months to 6 years
2) Senior After school - 6 years to 10 years
Children are exposed to different vendor activites such as dance, karate, yoga etc. They are also encouraged to complete their homework.
